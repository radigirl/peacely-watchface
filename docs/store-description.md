# Peacely Store Listing

## Short Description

A calm minimalist watch face with three soothing themes, configurable wellness metrics, and a clean, distraction-free design.

---

## Full Description

Peacely is a minimalist Garmin watch face designed for calm everyday use.

Built around readability and simplicity, it combines a large time display, soft color themes, and essential information without visual clutter. Choose between Sage, Warm, and Night themes, display either steps or heart rate, and switch between 24-hour and 12-hour time formats.

### Features

* Large, easy-to-read time display
* Battery percentage indicator
* Date display
* Daily steps or heart-rate metric
* Sage, Warm, and Night themes
* 24-hour and 12-hour time format support
* Lightweight minimalist layout
* Configurable settings through the Garmin Connect IQ app

### Device Support

Currently tested and supported on:

* Garmin Venu 2 Plus
* Garmin Venu 2
* Garmin Venu 2S

Additional device support may be added in future releases after testing and validation.

Peacely is designed for users who prefer a calm, clean watch face that focuses on the information they need most throughout the day.
